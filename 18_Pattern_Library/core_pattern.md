## 1. Traversal Pattern ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### প্রশ্ন কেমন হবে?

```text
Print all numbers

Find maximum

Find minimum

Sum of array

Average

Count vowels
```

---

### চিন্তা

```text
Need Traversal

↓

Visit Every Element Once
```

---

### Example

```text
1 5 2 9 3
```

Brain

```text
1

↓

5

↓

2

↓

9

↓

3
```
